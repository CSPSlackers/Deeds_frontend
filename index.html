Our Mission

Empowering Every Generation

At Doing Exceptional Deeds (D.A.D.), our mission is to uplift individuals and strengthen communities across generations through education-first programs that build confidence, character, accountability, and purpose.

We serve youth, young adults, student-athletes, and seniors through thoughtfully designed initiatives that support personal growth, life skills development, mentorship, and community engagement — meeting people where they are and guiding them toward their full potential.

Our work begins locally, extends nationally, and is built to scale globally — recognizing that the challenges facing today's youth and communities transcend borders, backgrounds, and generations.

Student Success
Doing Exceptional Deeds now offers a continuity-based Student Success Advisory model designed to support students from middle school through high school — providing consistent adult guidance, goal-setting, accountability, and reflection that follows students across grade levels and key transitions.

Rooted in high expectations, personal responsibility, and support delivered with care.

Who We Are

Doing. ExceptionAl. Deeds.

At Doing Exceptional Deeds, our mission is to uplift underserved communities through programs that inspire, empower, and address the needs of today while shaping a better tomorrow.

We believe in the potential of every child, and through sports, mental health initiatives, and community engagement, we strive to unlock that potential and provide the tools and support they need to thrive. By fostering resilience, teamwork, and personal growth, we empower tomorrow's youth to be leaders, dreamers, and changemakers.

Our commitment extends beyond youth. We embrace the wisdom and value of our seniors, creating meaningful connections that bridge generations through intergenerational programs that keep seniors engaged, connected, and inspired.

Our Programs Get Involved
Intergenerational Impact

Seniors & Community

We embrace the wisdom and value of our senior community members, creating meaningful intergenerational connections — especially during the holidays — that provide seniors opportunities to stay engaged, connected, and inspired.

Through our Senior Engagement Program, we partner seniors with youth in mentorship and community service, building bridges across generations that strengthen our entire community.
